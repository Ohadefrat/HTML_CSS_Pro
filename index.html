Gal Sasson

Hey, my name is Gal Sasson, age 24, lives in Tel Aviv, Israel. I'm a fashion design student at Shenkar college of Engineering and Design. During my studies at Shenkar I attended the following courses: Upcycal Fashion, Active wear, Swimwear, leather bags, garment inspired by uniform function, man wears inspired by a classic white shirt. My inspiration comes from my environment. I have great curiosity, love to explore and learn new things. I'm interested in shapes, proportion and nature of materials. My designs have a young and free spirit which I hope you feel while you wear them.
